November 2nd, 2013 | Posted by Alix in November Scifi Month - (13 Comments)

I am so excited to be a participating in Sci-Fi Month organized by the lovely Rinn of Rinn Reads! Every week for the month I’ll be posting sci-fi related posts every week for the month of November ranging from my favourite books, movies & tv shows to reviews! There’s also a surprise for you at the end (hint: it rhymes with biveaway ;)

1. Tell us a little bit about yourself?

I’m Alix, the “mysterious” half of A Novel Idea. ;) I’m 29 and Canadian. I’ve been blogging with Paola for less then year and I absolutely love it. Paola is my book sister from a different mother.

I work as a project manager, which keeps my insanely busy (but I love it!). I’ve had a passion for books since I was a kid as well a tv/movies. I am a total pop culture junky.

3. Why do you like sci-fi and what is your favourite thing about it?

I love how it allows your imagination to run wild. If you can imagine it, you can find it in sci-fi. It also allows us explore other worlds and futures and how our society and different societies might be. I also love how sci-fi has been able to be so relevant to your life and times even though it’s sci-fi. I also love just how broad of a genre it is! There are so many different kind of sci-fi and you can always find something.
