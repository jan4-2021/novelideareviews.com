I’m thrilled to feature Lindsay here, since she happens to be one of my favorite self-published authors and I’ve been a huge fan of Emperor’s Edge since reading the first book way back in 2011. If you haven’t met Amaranthe, Sicarius and the gang, that first installment is free for Kindle, Nook, and other eBook retailers… OR you can enter today’s giveaway for an eBook copy of the omnibus edition containing books 1-3! (The Emperor’s Edge, Dark Currents, Deadly Games) And a big thank you to Lindsay for taking time to do this interview while working hard on the second half of Forged In Blood!

An Interview with Lindsay Buroker

- I have to ask… how does it feel to be writing the final installment of EE? You’ve come such a long way with your characters and the books.Â
