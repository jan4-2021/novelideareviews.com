Happy Thanksgiving!

October 14th, 2013 | Posted by Alix in Thoughts & Musings

It’s Thanksgiving today here in Canada! TheÂ real Thanksgiving *coughcough*. While you’re reading this post, I will most likely be in the middle of preparing a turkey feast for my friends who are coming over to celebrate. And this after having celebrated with my family on Sunday! Needless to say, it’s a busy long weekend for me! :)

If you aren’t familiar with it, you can read about hereÂ but for me personally, Thanksgiving is a day to stop and appreciate all the good things I have in my life. Life can be so hectic (and has been for me these last few months) and I think it’s really important to stop, take a breath and reflect on the good you’ve experience during the past year and remind yourself to be grateful for what you have.

Thanksgiving has become one of my favourite holidays in recent years. It has everything I love: time family & friends, fall weather, apples, pumpkin pie and turkey! Since I love hosting and cooking, I always jump on the opportunity of gathering my love ones on this day and sharing good times and good food in terrific company.

If you practice the holiday, I hope you get to spend it with your loved ones. If you don’t, I ask that after you finish reading this post you take a couple of minutes to reflect on your life and give thanks for something you are grateful for; whether it’s something small or big.

Happy Turkey Day!
