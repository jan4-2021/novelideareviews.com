Instalover 1: April
Instalover 2: Gavin
Instalover 3: Colby

New student Gavin Wilson knew he was in love with April the moment he saw her chestnut ponytail swinging around the corner. He had to ask a new acquaintance for her name, and treasured the name April Woodley in his heart longing to meet her.

As his luck turned out, in his fifth period world history class there she was, so absorbed in reading a book that she couldn’t even make eye contact with him to solidify their instalove relationship! At least, not until class starts and the teacher introduces Gavin to the entire class. Then April’s eyes met his and he knew she felt it too, the instalove connection!

They chatted after class, and then again the next day, and the next, and when he asked her out on a date to a cafe at an indie bookstore in town, she said yes! But when they arrived at the bookstore cafe, Gavin noticed April locking her eyes with that of the barista, and it’s none other than fellow classmate Colby Smotherman!

DUN DUN DUN

Of course they have to talk to Colby to order their coffee and paninis, and Gavin made a show of paying for April, his eyes flashing Colby a threatening message, just daring him to interfere with their instalove. But Colby was sly, and when he delivered the coffee and paninis, he also brought a book for April to read, something he knew she’d love based on her based off her love for Teen Wolf and of course, there is a scene that involves a mud pie.

Much like the mud pie April and Colby shared so many years ago in kindergarten.

DUN DUN DUN

Who does April choose? Does instalove at first sight always win? Or sometimes, does a second-glance version of instalove end up being the most pure?
