It’s a Bird! It’s a Plane! It’s Sci-fi Month!

November 2nd, 2013 | Posted by Alix in November Scifi Month

I am so excited to be a participating in Sci-Fi Month organized by the lovely Rinn of Rinn Reads! Every week for the month I’ll be posting sci-fi related posts every week for the month of November ranging from my favourite books, movies & tv shows to reviews! There’s also a surprise for you at the end (hint: it rhymes with biveaway ;)

1. Tell us a little bit about yourself?

I’m Alix, the “mysterious” half of A Novel Idea. ;) I’m 29 and Canadian. I’ve been blogging with Paola for less then year and I absolutely love it. Paola is my book sister from a different mother.

I work as a project manager, which keeps my insanely busy (but I love it!). I’ve had a passion for books since I was a kid as well a tv/movies. I am a total pop culture junky.

2. How long have you been a fan of sci-fi?

I’ve been a fan of sci-fi since my early teens (so over 15 years)! I started enjoying television sci-fi shows (if it aired in the 90s and was sci-fi, I probably watched it!). FromÂ The X-Files toÂ Sliders to Doctor Who toÂ Stargate and so many more, those shows have all played a huge part in my life.

I started reading sci-fi only about 5 years ago. I might be late to party but have completely fallen in love with the written sci-fi genre and have consumed mass amount of it since!

3. Why do you like sci-fi and what is your favourite thing about it?

I love how it allows your imagination to run wild. If you can imagine it, you can find it in sci-fi. It also allows us explore other worlds and futures and how our society and different societies might be. I also love how sci-fi has been able to be so relevant to your life and times even though it’s sci-fi. I also love just how broad of a genre it is! There are so many different kind of sci-fi and you can always find something.

4. Favourite books/games/films/TV shows etc in the genre?

You’ll have to come back and check out my not one, but two! posts dedicated to my favourites. :)

5. What do you have planned for Sci-Fi Month?

I’ll be posting a variety of posts every week! I have a couple of favourite lists: for books and for tv/television, a thoughts and musing post about being a woman who enjoys sci-fi as well as a review ofÂ Ready Player One and the movie version ofÂ Ender’s Game, one of my all-time favourite books and recently released film! I’m gonna wrap up the month with a specialÂ not so secret surprise!

Whether you’re a sci-fi fan or novice, I hope you join me on this out of this world ride!
