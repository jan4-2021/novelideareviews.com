Love Triangles 101: The Final Exam

July 26th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Events | Love Triangles 101

Intro + Giveaway | Event Schedule

It’s been an amazing three weeks of Love Triangles 101! We’re starting to sound like a broken record, but THANK YOU SO MUCH to everyone who participated, whether you were one of our LT101 ‘guest lecturers’ or studiously taking notes in the back of the room ;D Today is the last day of the event and it’s crazy how quickly time flew by. We were treated to so many thoughtful (and hilarious) posts and discussions! You can still enter our giveaway, which ends at midnight, and we’ll also have a full round-up of all the posts so that you can catch up on anything you missed!

But first, there’s the final exam. Well, and a fun little prize too. No pain, no gain!

(…Oh, don’t look so surprised. Paola’s a teacher, OF COURSE there’s a final exam. That, and we’re both slightly diabolical in nature.)

Keep reading to find out what you need to do for an A+ in Love Triangles 101…

Below, we have provided a super fancy triangular diagram that will no doubt give you terrible flashbacks of A) geometry class or B) Twilight. This diagram is a love triangle we came up with to test your love triangle expertise, which we have complete faith in. We know you can do this. You’ve spent three weeks preparing! More than that, if you read lots of YA. So don’t stress! We’ve even provided certain dramatic details to increase your knowledge and boost creativity.

All you have to do is tell us how you think this love triangle should be resolved. Will it end in flames? Will it drown in tears? Will our characters be crushed by the dramavalanche?? (<– Borrowing this vocabulary word from you, Ana!) WILL IT CONTINUE FOR FIVE MORE BOOKS + 2 SPIN-OFFS???? The choice is yours.

By the way, we’ve got $10 to The Book Depository as a prize for our favorite answer! So go grab your #2 pencil! Bonus points for creativity! Psst… if you need some fresh ideas for love triangle resolutions, check out this post over at A Bookish Heart :)

** Please note that our regular giveaway policy still applies! **

Open for submissions until Sunday, 7/28 at 11PM PST; any responses posted after that will not be eligible for the giveaway.
