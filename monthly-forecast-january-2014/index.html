Monthly Forecast: January 2014

January 1st, 2014 | Posted by Paola in Announcements | Monthly Forecast

Wondering what kind of shenanigans to expect from us this month? Of course you are. Hereâs the January 2014 forecast for A Novel Idea!

Before I launch into the forecast for January, I just want to say that 2013 was an AMAZING year for us and for the blog — we met so many new friends and it’s been so awesome being part of the community. It was really the first year I was serious about blogging, and it was also the year that Alix and I went from being pen pals to co-bloggers. I’m grateful for all the bloggers we’re now friends with (Duchesses! A special slice of cake for you three! :D) and all the readers who have taken the time to follow A Novel Idea, comment on posts, & support us even when we’re too busy to provide you with regular shenanigans. YOU GUYS ARE THE BEST EVER.

We’re looking forward to 2014!! From both of us here at A Novel Idea, Happy New Year and best of luck to all of you! We love you! And now for the rest of our forecast…

Alix is on vacation!

Alix is adventuring in Cambodia right now! She’ll be back in mid-January, classier and more awesome than ever. Before she left for her trip, we talked about our plans for the blog this coming year and jointly lamented the fact that we were both so busy in the last few months of 2013 — it was really tough juggling the blog and our jobs (and life in general) during that time. We are SO SORRY that we have to keep making the same excuse over and over again! But you know, if we started to view the reading/reviewing/blogging process as a job in itself, it would take away so much of the enjoyment. So we’re going to continue as we are, doing something we love and have fun with, without taking it all so seriously that we lose sight of why we’re here.

Our very big surprise announcement!

This discussion did serve as the catalyst for our very big New Year’s announcement, though: WE’RE ADDING A NEW CO-BLOGGER THIS MONTH!! The dynamic duo is turning into a trio :) Words can’t express how very excited we are about adding a third blogger to A Novel Idea this month. She’s amazing and you’re going to love her! Come back on Friday when we introduce her to all of you! Eek, I’m so excited!!

On that note, #2PersonBookClub will now become #3PersonBookClub! We aren’t choosing a book this month because Alix is gone until mid-January, and we haven’t decided yet if we’ll be reading anything for February due to how busy it’s gonna get with a certain event we’re running that month… (more below!) … so for now, that feature is on hiatus. But it’ll be back eventually, we promise!

The Thirteenth Bride

I’ve been babbling about my collab writing project with Tory and Daire for a while now, but get used to it cause I’m so thrilled about this story and writing it with such incredible people! You can now follow along with the project at its official home, where we’ll post all the new chapters and updates.

Instalove 101 Sign-Ups

Some of you may remember a little event we ran last year called Love Triangles 101, when we spent three whole weeks exploring the very polarizing topic of triangular love — the good, the bad, and the ugly. We were joined in this endeavor by a fabulous group of fellow bloggers who wrote posts ranging from humorous to introspective, and it was such a great experience that we decided to make it an annual thing. This year, we’re covering another extremely common YA trope: the miracle (disaster??) of Instalove! The event will start on Valentine’s Day 2014 and sign-ups begin this month on January 14th! PLEASE join us for the fun! Comment if you’re interested in signing up early and we’ll contact you via email just like we did last year!

Whatâs going on for you this month? Do you want to join us in our monthly shenanigans?? Inquiring minds want to know!
