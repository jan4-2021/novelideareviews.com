For Review:

I haven’t finished The Darkest Minds yet, but I’m already convinced I need the sequel. I’m hooked! It’s a big departure from Brightly Woven, which I just finished a few days ago, but definitely not in a bad way. Plus, Alexandra Bracken is on the list of authors scheduled to be at the Vegas Valley Book Festival in November so I HAVE to read everything she’s ever written now, haha. A huge thank you to Disney Book Group for the ARC!

  • Never Fade (The Darkest Minds, #2) by Alexandra Bracken (Disney Book Group)

Library Finds:

Soooo I picked up When You Were Here just yesterday and I’m already done reading! I enjoyed it very much, definitely would recommend even to reluctant YA contemporary converts like me. Review coming soon :) I also have Just One Day, which I’m looking forward to since I loved Gayle Forman’s If I Stay when I read it a few months ago. And if you’ve been paying attention, Alix and I chose The Demon King by Cinda Williams Chima as our #2PersonBookClub selection for June, and The Exiled Queen is the second book in that series. We both loved the first one and are both launching into the rest of the series now!

  • When You Were Here by Daisy Whitney
  • Just One Day (Just One Day, #1) by Gayle Forman
  • The Exiled Queen (Seven Realms, #2) by Cinda Williams Chima

That’s it for me this week! What’s in your book haul today? Whether you have one book or many, happy reading to you!!
