For Review

Thanks to the publishers & NetGalley for the first three; I found Loki’s Wolves in my mailbox earlier this week from Little, Brown & Co and Shelf Awareness! (It’s my first ever physical book to receive as an ARC so it was kinda like Christmas, haha.)

  • The Night Rainbow by Claire King (Bloomsbury USA)
  • The Loop by Shandy Lawson (Disney Book Group)
  • The Rose Throne by Mette Ivie Harrison (Egmont USA)
  • Loki’s Wolves by K.L. Armstrong & M.A. Marr (Little, Brown & Co)

Library Finds

We’re reading The Map of Time at my book club this month (the theme was Time Travel) so I picked it up at the library! Paired with the 600-page tome called The Diviners by Libba Bray, it makes for A LOT of reading to do this month, haha.

  • The Madman’s Daughter by Megan Shepherd
  • The Map of Time by Felix J. Palma
  • Extremely Loud & Incredibly Close by Jonathan Safran Foer
  • The Silence of Bonaventure Arrow by Rita Leganski

Purchased

The US Kindle Store put all seven Narnia books on sale earlier this week so I grabbed the two I like best for $1.99 each. It seems that most people like the other books more than these, but as someone who actually has never been fond of the Pevensies, I’ve always found myself more drawn to Books 1 and 2. (I don’t know why I was the one kid in the world who could care less about Peter, Susan, Edmund & Lucy… LOL) Oh, and I’m VERY excited about The Rook, which I heard about on another book blog last weekend.

  • The Rook by Daniel O’Malley
  • The Magician’s Nephew by C.S. Lewis
  • The Horse and His Boy by C.S. Lewis

That’s it for me this week! What’s in your haul? Share your link and I’ll be sure to drop by!
