For Review:

This one already released earlier this month but was still on NetGalley when I went looking for it so I requested an ARC. I’ve heard some interesting things and I’ve always loved the idea of parallel worlds (my favorite example being Philip Pullman’s His Dark Materials series) so I’m looking forward to A Corner of White! Plus the synopsis says it’s going to be funny, and I like funny.

  • A Corner of White by Jaclyn Moriarty (Scholastic)

Library Finds

Yay, finally my turn to read Out of the Easy! Don’t even ask how long I was on the wait list for it… let’s just say it was ridiculous. Here’s hoping it was worth the wait! From the reviews I’ve seen, it seems like it will be :) Ah, and I also got The Flame in the Mist, which we posted a trailer reveal for not long ago.

  • Out of the Easy by Ruta Sepetys
  • The Demon King (Seven Kingdoms #1) by Cinda Williams Chima
  • The Flame in the Mist by Kit Grindstaff

Purchased

Um, this book blew my mind. I am still thinking about it right now, 2 days later. It reminded me why Fantasy (especially High Fantasy) has always been my absolute favorite genre. The minute I finished reading the eBook from the library, I switched to my browser and bought the Kindle edition so I can have it forever. (Most of the books I buy have to be books I’ve already read and love enough to spend money on, as a rule.) I desperately want the next book in the series but I already know if I buy it and start reading, I’ll never get to anything else this weekend. So I’m staying away for the time being, but it REALLY hurts. /ugly cry Anyway, my review for this is coming up next week! I can’t promise it will be coherent. I haven’t recovered yet.

  • Finnikin of the Rock (The Lumatere Chronicles #1) by Melina Marchetta
