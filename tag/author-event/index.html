Recap: Vegas Valley Book Festival 2013

November 6th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Events - (1 Comments)

Hi everyone! Paola here, writing to you from the edge of the NaNoWriMo abyss~ I’m on hiatus this month while all this crazy novel-writing business is going on, but I really wanted to recap the awesome weekend I just had with my even more awesome friend Charlene (Bookish Whimsy) at Vegas Valley Book Festival 2013! So I’m stepping away from my word processor for a second to spam you with pictures and flip out over all the great authors we spotted out in the wild on Saturday. I feel like it’s my civic duty. (Also maybe I’m kinda going cross-eyed after staring at my unfinished novel for so long… err….)

I brought my fancy camera because obviously I’m a professional at this memory-making business. Pay attention. Now, we got there pretty early — like 45 mins before the first YA panel was supposed to start — and had lots of moments of serendipity such as mystically finding the parking garage when we weren’t even looking for it. This is how we knew it was meant to be. (Btw, Charlene and I had the best, most fantastic three-day slumber party EVER.) For once the weather was actually GORGEOUS in Las Vegas, something that only happens once a year when the planets align. The festival was all outdoors, so this was fortunate. We ran into our friend Stacie, too! She had the cutest rolling suitcase ever… girl meant business, okay?

Dark Days Tour Recap

September 26th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Event Recap | Events - (33 Comments)

So for my first ever author event, I drove 3 hours and 40 minutes to LA for the Crown of Midnight launch party (also lovingly referred to around here as The Explosion of Sarah Maas Fangirl Feels and The Time Charlene & I Turned the Entire Event Into a Melina Marchetta Flailing Session) and I had a BLAST. This time, however, I only had to drive 15 minutes to the Barnes & Noble near my house for the first stop on the Fall 2013 Dark Days Tour! Which, of course, was an incredible experience.

As you can see from this lovely poster I picked up at the bookstore, the authors on tour were special guest Tera Lynn Childs (Sweet Venom), Madeleine Roux (Asylum), Rae Carson (The Bitter Kingdom), Michelle Gagnon (Don’t Look Now), Mindy McGinnis (Not A Drop To Drink) and Sherry Thomas (The Burning Sky). I was mainly there to fangirl incessantly over Sherry Thomas because I’d just finished reading her book on Tuesday and still hadn’t fully recovered from the awesome. I think meeting her only caused a relapse and now all I want to do is read it again, actually. You can hear me gush over it in my review over here!

To kick the event off, the authors all took some time to tell the audience about their book. Everyone volunteered Sherry Thomas to go first, so of course she completely proves my theory that we’re kindred spirits by announcing, “My book is about cross-dressing.” Which it is!!! I’m in the audience like, YES!!! *squeaking with joy*
