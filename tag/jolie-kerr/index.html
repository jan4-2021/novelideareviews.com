THIS BOOK. IS HILARIOUS. Like I can’t even describe to you how funny it is, not to mention so so so so SO relevant to my daily existence. I’m definitely nowhere near being domesticated in any sense, and the very idea of cleaning is enough to completely demotivate me from getting out of bed in the morning. Uhh, that’s probably why my husband was staring at me when I was so excited about the opportunity to absorb Jolie Kerr’s infinite wisdom — I mean, it would be more logical for me to run away from this book screaming bloody murder. But the thing is, if you’re as much of a cleaning-phobe as I am, then YOU WILL LOVE THIS BOOK. And also, you need it. Desperately. Because it will make cleaning actually sound… /GASP/… kind of fun. And hysterical.

Likewise, if you looooooove cleaning and it’s your favorite thing ever in the whole world, you’re gonna love this book too. Seriously. It’s for everyone. I firmly believe this.
