Alix is in her late twenties, a project manager by day and bookworm-in-training by night. She has always been a avid reader, receiving awards from her high school for having taken out of the most books. Alix loves reading in English and French. She will sometimes pick up a French book in its English translation if the cover is prettier (she also really likes pretty things) and has her bookshleves organized by book cover colour. A fan of all things British (the royalty, Downtown Abbey, tea, Colin Firth), Alix will forever hold Jane Austen in a special place in her heart as her novels were the first works of classic English fiction she read as in impressionable teenager. Alix is mostly reading YA (enough with the love triangles already!) and romance novels (a genre people are often too quick to dismiss) these days. She can be found reading into the wee hours of the morning, spotted with her nose buried in a book on her daily commute and browsing books on Goodreads.
