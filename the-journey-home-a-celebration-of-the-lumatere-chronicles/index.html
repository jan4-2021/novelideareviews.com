The Journey Home: A Celebration of the Lumatere Chronicles

September 17th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Collaborations | Events | The Journey Home

Intro Post @ Bookish Whimsy

A Special Bulletin From Your Finnikin Ambassador

As most of you are well aware, I have made it my mission in life to harass convince everyone in the universe to read an incredible high fantasy series called The Lumatere Chronicles (Finnikin of the Rock, Froi of the Exiles, Quintana of Charyn), by Melina Marchetta. I can’t even begin to express to you how much I love these books — they leave me an inarticulate, sobbing, comatose mess. After finally reading Finnikin of the Rock earlier this year (WHERE HAD IT BEEN ALL MY LIFE?!) I subsequently spread the Finnikin fever to several friends, happy customers all, and now we gush about the series frequently. One of those friends is the lovely Charlene over at Bookish Whimsy, and we’ve teamed up to put together an entire event dedicated to our shared obsession. You can get more details from our intro post at Charlene’s blog, but basically we’ll be hosting a read-along of the first book as well as three weeks of posts from all over the book blogging world on The Lumatere Chronicles!

Tentative Scheduling:

The Event runs from October 7th – 27th
Finnikin of the Rock Read-Along: October 7th-20th. (Thatâs two weeks to read 400pg)
October 7th-13th: Prologue and Chapters 1-15
October 14th-20th: Chapters 16-30
(Scheduled posts by participating bloggers will be added at Bookish Whimsy as they are received.)

Whether you’re new to the series or love it as much as we do, we’d love it if you joined us on our journey home to Lumatere! (Well, technically I never left, but yeah.) We want to laugh, cry, and be reduced to complete train wrecks with you~ So please do sign up for the event, scheduled for October 7th-27th! Just fill out the form here or at Bookish Whimsy. And be sure to grab the shiny badge!

See you in Lumatere!

Paola (and Charlene!)
