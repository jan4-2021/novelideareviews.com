Good question! For me, if a character I was attached to ends up dying and I find out before I reach the end, I DO flip out and get upset and et al etc. But pretty much every single time, I keep reading anyway. (The handful of times I’ve peeked, anyway!) I may be really depressed reading the story knowing that this person is going to die, but in my (apparently really bizarre) view, at least I was prepared for it. I can actually concentrate on what I’m reading now that I’m prepared for the inevitable. Which I realize probably makes 0 sense to most readers out there, but it’s how my brain works!
