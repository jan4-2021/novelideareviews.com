The Monthly Forecast: August 2013

August 1st, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Monthly Forecast

Wondering what kind of shenanigans to expect from us this month? Of course you are. Here’s the August forecast for A Novel Idea!

A Novel Idea Turns 5!

Believe it or not, this little corner of the internet has been around since 2008! For the first four years of its existence, A Novel Idea was more of a personal blog and I never promoted it as a full-fledged book blog. And then I decided to come back and make book blogging a bigger part of my life, which is honestly one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. Thanks to this blog, I’ve met so many amazing people, become better friends with my fabulous co-blogger Alix, and now I pretty much ALWAYS have someone to talk about books with! In honor of our 5th birthday, we’re unveiling a shiny redesign of the blog on Monday, August 5th. We hope you find some time to stop by and tell us what you think! (Psst… we may be giving away some books…) While we’re on the subject, if we’re down for maintenance a lot over the next few days, don’t panic. AWESOMENESS IS HAPPENING.

August ARCs

August is packed with quite a few exciting new releases, some of which we were lucky enough to read and review in advance! Here are some future reviews to look forward to:

  • The Boy Who Could See Demons by Carolyn Jess-Cooke
  • Emma: A Latter-Day Tale by Rebecca H. Jamison
  • Between the Devil and the Deep Blue Sea by April Genevieve Tucholke
  • The Bone Season by Samantha Shannon
  • Love in the Time of Global Warming by Francesca Lia Block

Gasp! A collaboration with Gypsy Reviews!

So, so excited to announce an upcoming collab with our very good friend (and Paola’s book twin) Charlotte over at Gypsy Reviews! We won’t say anything more about it, except that at some point there will most likely be a long treatise on why Mulan is a Certified Badass. (Duh.) Charlotte is currently on hiatus, but if you aren’t already following her blog… YOU SHOULD BE. I sense she’ll be back with a vengeance ;D The event is scheduled for August 20-22 so stay tuned!!

Our August Wishlist

We don’t do Waiting on Wednesday posts, but that doesn’t mean we’re any less covetous than the rest. Here are two books we’re dying to get our hands on this month! (Paola’s shouldn’t be a surprise to anyone…) What about you? Are you looking forward to these too??

Alix’s Pick: The Passion of the Purple Plumeria (Pink Carnation, #10) by Lauren Willig

I adore the Pink Carnation series by Lauren Willig! I read the first 8 books in over the span of 2 months and have ever since been not so patiently waiting for a new book in the series year. Set in England during the Napoleonic wars, the Pink Carnation series follows the various hijinks of delightful characters who are all in one way or another involved in spying for Her Majesty. Funny, witty, smart and just overall wonderful, I cannot recommend this series enough and am SUPER EXCITED for âThe Passion of the Purple Plumeriaâ!

Paola’s Pick: Crown of Midnight (Throne of Glass, #2) by Sarah J. Maas

What, I told you it shouldn’t be a surprise! Although I have yet to fully recover from getting rejected for the ARC on Netgalley two days before my birthday (thanks Charlotte and Alix for comforting me during my spiral into despair), I just found out Sarah J. Maas will be in L.A. for her U.S. book tour… AND I’M TOTALLY DRIVING OVER THERE!!! It’s about 4 hours away from Vegas. Plus, WORTH IT. Is anyone else going?? I’m planning to attend her signing on August 27th! If you see a crazy girl in the corner flailing her arms and sobbing into her copy of Throne of Glass, it’s probably me. Additional clue: there will probably be a fairly exasperated guy standing at a safe distance, wondering why he married me and subsequently found himself dragged to Los Angeles so I can fulfill a lifelong dream. HAHA. TOUGH BREAKS, HUSBAND.

What’s going on in your world this month? Do tell!
