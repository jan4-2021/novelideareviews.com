The Round-Robin Project

August 14th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Collaborations | Features | Round-Robin Project

Obligatory Disclaimer: The following shenanigans are being brought to you by Paola @ A Novel Idea and Tory @ Sleeping Latte! (Run away now while you can!)

PAOLA: So as avid readers & reviewers, we tend to see a lot of things in books that drive us up the wall. This happens a lot to me while reading YA. Love triangles, Instalove, et al etc… there are just so many things I end up wishing I could change when Iâm reading a book that has these elements included. I think that this is how a lot of avid readers end up also becoming writers. We have all these smartass ideas for how to fix things, donât we? So letâs fix them! By writing our own!

TORY: We got this idea from the final exam of Love Triangles 101, where several readers submitted their “essays” that ranged from super hilarious (lethal sneezing) to super level headed! Then because I (The Sleeping Latte who is typing this in half-awake state) thought that was a really, awesomely fun idea. Paola suggested a Round Robin Writing event! Some shenanigans went down (oh man, sorry Paola, I’m laughing at my own autocorrects lol) (Paola: itâs so awesome lololol) thereafter that resulted in us being here! Now! Because we need you and your input to see if there’s enough interest in this! :’D

PAOLA: Basically everyone interested would be invited to group together and get started with some initial worldbuilding. We decided this would be more fun to do together and that way weâre all experts on the world weâve created and how it works. Then, we would start taking turns writing the chapters, one chapter at a time, and each chapter hosted at a different blog. If you wrote the chapter, you would host the story on your blog that day, and so on.

TORY: Right now, what we need to know is how many of you would be interested in participating in this event! It would probably take a while, as we are likely to give a week for each chapter to be posted. Therefore we are thinking of December as a possible starting month because November is NaNoWriMo so we’re guessing some of us might be a little busy (in my case, busy whining and ripping my hair out for words ha…ha…ha). Also, it’d be nice to know what kind of story you guys want to write, such as a parody of a genre or an entirely new story!

PAOLA: But not contemporary because neither Tory nor I is any good at it.

TORY: Hahaha yup, unless you want your character killed off :D

PAOLA: OR ALL THE CHARACTERS KILLED OFF >:D

So anyway, here’s everything you need to know about our crackpot scheme:

  • We’re looking for fellow bloggers/aspiring writers who are interested in collaborating on a story together!
  • Everyone participating will alternate writing chapters, with each new chapter posting on the writer’s blog that week.
  • There will be about a week in between chapters to (hopefully) give enough time for writing.
  • We’ll all do the worldbuilding together, but the plot will not be outlined. We want to see where it goes!
  • There will be no minimum/maximum chapter length BUT there will be a chapter limit so that the story eventually ends.
  • If you’re interested, fill out the form so we can contact you with further details!
