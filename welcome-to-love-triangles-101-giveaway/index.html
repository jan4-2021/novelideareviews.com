Welcome to Love Triangles 101! + GIVEAWAY!

July 8th, 2013 | Posted by Paola in Giveaway | Love Triangles 101

July 8th – 26th, 2013

Welcome to Love Triangles 101 — a geometry lesson on perhaps one of the most polarizing topics in YA these days. What is it about the love triangle that inspires such a wide range of reactions? Why do some love triangles work, while others leave us headdesking ourselves into oblivion? Through the many awesome posts going live during this event, we hope to examine our feelings about love triangles and their intricacies. Questions will be asked, answers will be provided, opinions will be aired, and new perspectives will be presented. I honestly can’t wait.

Alix and I are beyond thrilled at the response we got about this event. We actually had to extend it by a full week to accommodate the number of fantastic bloggers out there who answered our call, which means you get practically a full month ofÂ  incredible posts to read! We’d really like to take a moment to thank those bloggers again for their support of Love Triangles 101, and for the awesome topics they came up with… you guys are the best. EVER.

We hope to announce dates and times for LT101 Twitter chats taking place this month; still trying to sort out all the various timezones in an effort to include as many of you guys as possible in the discussion! We’ll keep you updated here and on Twitter!

To kick off the event, I’d like to get that discussion going with a question for all of you:

Which love triangle in YA do you have the strongest feelings for? (Love, hate, indifference, confusion — whatever your strong feeling is, we want to hear about it!)

Answering this question in the comments also happens to be one way to win our special LT101 prize: a $20 giftcard to The Book Depository! Hey, you could pick something that’s Certified Love Triangle Free…! As always, please read the rules before entering; bloggers participating in LT101 are more than welcome to enter as well, and please link the giveaway to your readers if you want! We’re all about sharing the love~
